# Replit Prompt — TCG All-in-One Website

Build a stunning, modern landing page + multi-page website for **TCG All-in-One** — the ultimate digital platform for Trading Card Game collectors and players.

## What the product is

TCG All-in-One is an all-in-one app that centralizes everything a TCG collector/player needs:
- **Collection Management**: Track your cards, organize by set/rarity/condition, track set completion %, scan cards with camera
- **Market Data & Valuation**: Real-time card prices, historical price charts, portfolio value, personal ROI, price alerts
- **Deck Builder**: Build decks with legality checks, synergy analysis, card suggestions from your collection, public sharing
- **Social & Community**: Public profiles, browse other collections, follow collectors, compare progress, achievement badges & milestones
- **Marketplace (coming soon)**: Buy/sell cards, smart matching, secure trading, rated profiles

Supported games: **Pokémon TCG, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Magic: The Gathering, One Piece Card Game**

The tagline: *"Turn your TCG collection into an intelligent portfolio, a social asset, and a competitive ecosystem."*

## Visual Direction & Aesthetic

This is the most important part. The design must feel like **a premium manga/anime-infused trading card universe**. Think:

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Dark-themed UI as the base (deep blacks, rich dark blues, charcoal) — like opening a premium booster pack at midnight
- Vibrant neon accent colors that pop: electric blue, holographic purple, gold foil, hot pink — inspired by holographic/rainbow rare cards
- The feeling of a high-end Japanese card shop meets a futuristic collector's vault
- Energy and excitement — this is NOT a boring fintech dashboard, it's a collector's paradise

### Visual Effects & Details
- **Holographic / foil card effects**: Shimmering gradients that shift on hover, iridescent surfaces, rainbow reflections — make elements feel like actual holo cards
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass panels with backdrop blur over dark backgrounds
- **Particle effects or floating elements**: Subtle sparkles, energy orbs, or floating card silhouettes in hero sections
- **Card fanning / spread animations**: Cards that fan out, flip, rotate in 3D on scroll or hover
- **Glow effects**: Soft neon glows behind buttons, cards, and key UI elements
- **Manga-style accents**: Speed lines, dynamic angles, bold typography with slight Japanese typographic influence
- **Grain/noise texture overlay**: Subtle film grain for that premium editorial feel

### Typography
- Bold, impactful display font for headlines (think: Bebas Neue, Oswald, or something with manga energy)
- Clean modern sans-serif for body text (DM Sans, Satoshi, or similar)
- Some accents in a stylized/decorative font for section labels or badges

### Layout & Composition
- Hero section: Full-screen, cinematic — a dramatic card spread or holographic card floating in space with particles, the logo huge and proud, a glowing CTA button
- Feature sections: Use card-like containers with rounded corners, holographic borders, and hover effects
- Asymmetric layouts with overlapping elements, diagonal sections, and depth layers
- Scroll-triggered animations: elements slide in, cards flip, stats count up
- Mobile-first responsive design

## Tech Preferences
- React (Next.js if possible) with TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- Framer Motion for animations
- Any cool libraries you see fit for the holographic/particle effects (three.js, tsparticles, etc.)
- Responsive and performant

## Business Model Context
- Freemium: Free tier with core features, Premium tier with advanced analytics, custom alerts, exclusive badges, marketplace advantages
- Target audience: Serious TCG collectors and players aged 16-40, digital-native, community-driven
- Tone: Premium but accessible, exciting but trustworthy, gamer-friendly but not childish

## What I DON'T want
- Generic SaaS landing page with boring gradients
- Corporate/enterprise vibes
- Flat, lifeless UI with no personality
- Stock photos — use illustrated/stylized visuals or CSS art instead
- White/light theme as default (dark mode is the primary theme)

## Summary
Make it feel like the website itself is a rare holographic card — premium, eye-catching, collectible. Every section should make a TCG collector think "this was built BY collectors, FOR collectors." The website should be as exciting as pulling a chase card from a booster pack.
