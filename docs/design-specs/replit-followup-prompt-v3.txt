## 1. SUPPORTED UNIVERSES SECTION — Complete Redesign (Top Priority)

This is the weakest section on the entire site. Right now it's just 4 dark rectangles with colored text. It needs to be one of the most visually exciting moments on the page.

**New design:**
- Each game gets a large interactive card/tile (not just text labels)
- Each tile should have:
  - The game's **official color identity** as a gradient background glow (Pokémon = yellow/red, Yu-Gi-Oh! = orange/gold, MTG = dark blue/gold, One Piece = red/straw-hat vibes)
  - A **silhouette or stylized illustration** of an iconic card back or symbol for that game (don't use copyrighted images — use CSS art, geometric shapes, or abstract representations that evoke each game's aesthetic)
  - The game name in its **characteristic style** (Pokémon in bold rounded, Yu-Gi-Oh! in angular/sharp, MTG in serif/elegant, One Piece in adventurous/playful)
  - A subtle **card count** like "12,400+ cards tracked" (placeholder numbers)
- On hover: the tile glows brighter, slightly scales up (1.05), and the background gradient shifts
- The section title should say something like "FOUR UNIVERSES. ONE VAULT." instead of just "Supported Universes"
- Add a subtle animated particle/sparkle effect behind the tiles

## 2. BRING BACK THE ACHIEVEMENTS / BADGES SECTION

The previous version had a badges showcase (Vault Founder, Master Trader, Set Completer, Whale Status) that was removed. Bring it back and make it even better.

**Design:**
- Section title: "COLLECT MORE THAN CARDS" or "EARN YOUR STATUS"
- Show 6-8 achievement badges in a horizontal scroll or grid
- Each badge is a circular emblem with:
  - A unique icon in the center (trophy, flame, crown, diamond, lightning bolt, star, etc.)
  - A colored ring around it matching its rarity tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Holographic)
  - The badge name below (e.g., "VAULT FOUNDER", "MASTER TRADER", "SET KING", "WHALE STATUS", "DECK ARCHITECT", "COMMUNITY LEGEND")
  - A rarity label below the name (COMMON, RARE, EPIC, LEGENDARY, MYTHIC)
- The holographic/legendary badges should have a **shimmer animation** — a CSS gradient that sweeps across them on loop, like a holographic card catching light
- On hover: badge slightly rotates in 3D (like tilting a holo card) and the glow intensifies
- Add a small tagline below: "14 unique badges to unlock. Let your profile tell your story."

Place this section BETWEEN the feature cards and the pricing section.
